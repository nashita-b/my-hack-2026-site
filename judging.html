Judging & prizes

Every team presents to a panel of judges on Day 2. Here's what they're looking for, and what's on the line.

Awards

$2,000 prize pool.

Judged separately by division — High School and College each award their own 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place team.

1st place
$500
per division
2nd place
$300
per division
3rd place
$200
per division
How you're judged

100 points, four categories.

Scores are a general range within each band, not an exact science. Download the judge's PDF ↗

1. Impact & Relevance

Max 35 pts
No Credit
0 pts
No clear connection to a real community need.
Developing
~12 pts
Addresses a real need, but the benefit is vague or small-scale.
Effective
~24 pts
Clearly helps a specific group — you can picture who'd use it.
Excellent
35 pts
Meaningfully benefits the Muslim community in a specific, believable, real-world way.

2. Innovation

Max 25 pts
No Credit
0 pts
Existing solution with no meaningful changes.
Developing
~8 pts
Adds a small twist to an established approach.
Effective
~17 pts
A genuinely new angle, or a fresh combination of ideas.
Excellent
25 pts
An approach or perspective that stands out as original.

3. Presentation & Design

Max 20 pts
No Credit
0 pts
Hard to understand what it does or why it matters.
Developing
~7 pts
Some effort, but navigation or the pitch is confusing.
Effective
~14 pts
Clear, well-organized demo; a few extra instructions still needed.
Excellent
20 pts
Clean, compelling design and pitch — no extra explanation needed.

4. Technical Execution & Feasibility

Max 20 pts
No Credit
0 pts
Little to nothing works; core features missing or broken.
Developing
~7 pts
Some features work, but major pieces are missing or broken.
Effective
~14 pts
Most core features work as intended; realistically finishable soon.
Excellent
20 pts
Fully functional — could go live with minimal extra work.

Optional bonus — AI Use

Up to +5 pts

Scored on AI in the final product only — not on whether the team used AI tools like Claude or Copilot while building. No AI at all is not penalized; a project can still score the full 100 without it.

0 ptsNo AI in the product, or AI is present but adds nothing real.
3 ptsAI adds genuine value to the experience.
5 ptsAI is thoughtfully integrated and is a real reason the project works as well as it does.
